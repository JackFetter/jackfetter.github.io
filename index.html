  • 🖨 PDF Assistant

    As part of my job, I publish pricing schedule sheets for our products out of an Excel worksheet I created. After inserting formulas, I export the worksheetsheet to two seperate PDF files for 24 and 36 month contract terms. I had trouble merging these files without the use of a third-party online service. To help streamline this workflow, I built a Python 3 script which uses PyPDF2 to merge the two PDF files.
